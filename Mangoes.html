About Mango

Mangoes are the most widely used Fruit in Indian houses. It is also called as the King of Fruits. Mangoes are seasonal. They are available only in the months of April, May and June and sometimes uptp July.
Mangoes are available both in the raw form as well as ripe form. Raw mangoes are used to prepare curries in south India. They are of various varieties

Benisha: Available in large sizes

Raspuri: Small sized mangoes with rich juiciness

Alphonso: Sweetest and pretiest of the mangoes
